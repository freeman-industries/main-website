LeSalon

This on-demand service lets you book manicures and pedicures by trained professionals in the comfort of your own home.

LeSalon approached us in 2015, with a product in need of serious work. They had moved fast with their product, but had accrued a lot of technial debt along the way - this finally reached a point where they could not progress any further without serious engineering across their whole stack.

Freeman Industries migrated their Rails API to Node.js, rebuilding every aspect of it along the way - improved functionality and more than 75% code coverage in tests.

We have also done a lot of work on their Meteor web app, eliminating bugs, improving performance and loads of visual & UX work.
