Tippler

Tippler delivers the most sought-after wines imported from all over the world to your doorstep within 90 minutes of ordering. Currently operating in Shanghai, and launching in other major Chinese cities soon!

Tech stack: Moltin, Alicloud, YooPay, AngularJS, Node.js/Express

We built the entire app, from design and experience, all the way to scaling and delivery. Moltin's ecommerce backend reduced our development time by half.

This was our first time shipping code in Mainland China and first time using YooPay and Alicloud - both Chinese, mobile payments and cloud hosting respectively.
