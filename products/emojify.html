Emojify

Filters are boring... Now there's a new way to add some fun to your photos! Emojify makes it easy to quickly add emoji to your photos and share them with your friends.

We created and launched Emojify as an exercise in building, shipping and promoting a free iOS app from scratch.

6 months after release, we have achieved 25,000 installs, trended on Reddit and Product Hunt and have been part of minor press features in Wales and The Netherlands.
